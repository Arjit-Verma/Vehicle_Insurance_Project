POLICY 1

Overview:

Comprehensive coverage is a type of auto insurance that covers damage to your vehicle from incidents other than collisions. It's sometimes called "comp" or "other than collision" insurance. This policy offers extensive protection for your vehicle, providing peace of mind knowing that you're financially protected in various scenarios.

Coverage Details:

  • Accidents: Comprehensive coverage protects your vehicle from damages resulting from accidents. Whether it's a fender bender or a major collision, this policy will cover the cost of repairing or replacing your vehicle.

Cost:

The premium for comprehensive coverage typically depends on various factors, including the value of your vehicle, your driving history, deductible amount, and location. The cost may vary, but for a policy offering comprehensive coverage at 25000rs/year, you can expect comprehensive protection for your vehicle at a reasonable rate.

Conclusion:

Investing in a comprehensive coverage policy provides you with extensive protection for your vehicle beyond what basic liability insurance offers. With coverage for accidents, theft, vandalism, weather events, and more, this policy offers peace of mind knowing that you're financially safeguarded in various situations.
