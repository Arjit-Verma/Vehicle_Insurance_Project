POLICY 3

Overview:

The Personal Injury Protection (PIP) Policy is a type of auto insurance that provides coverage for medical expenses and lost wages incurred by you and your passengers due to injuries sustained in a car accident. It is a no-fault insurance, meaning that regardless of who is at fault in the accident, this policy will provide coverage for medical expenses and lost wages.

Coverage Details:

  • Medical Expenses Coverage: This aspect of the policy covers the medical expenses incurred by you and your passengers for injuries sustained in a car accident. It includes hospital bills, doctor's fees, surgical costs, prescription medications, and other necessary medical treatments.
  • Lost Wages Coverage: If you or your passengers are unable to work due to injuries sustained in a car accident, this policy will provide coverage for lost wages. It compensates for the income lost during the recovery period, helping you maintain financial stability.

Cost:

The premium for the Personal Injury Protection (PIP) Policy is typically priced at 15000rs/year. The cost may vary depending on factors such as the coverage limits, deductible amount, your driving history, and the insurance provider's pricing structure.

Conclusion:

The Personal Injury Protection (PIP) Policy offers essential financial protection for you and your passengers in the event of injuries sustained in a car accident. By opting for this policy, you ensure that you have coverage for medical expenses and lost wages, regardless of who is at fault in the accident, providing peace of mind and financial security for you and your loved ones.
